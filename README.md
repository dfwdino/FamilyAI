##FamilyAI -- A Safe AI Chat for Your Kids

FamilyAI is a locally hosted AI chat application built for families who want to give their kids access to AI without sacrificing privacy or control. It leverages Ollama to process all AI requests, which means nothing leaves your house and no personal information is shared with any third-party service.
The Kids area gives children a focused and guided chat experience. The system prompt is pre-configured to keep conversations appropriate and educational, and kids are not able to delete their chat history.

The Parents area gives you full visibility into everything your kids have been asking. You can review flagged chats that contain invalid words or topics, and you can customize the system prompt to keep your kids focused on whatever matters most to you -- whether that is schoolwork, a specific subject, or general safe browsing behavior.

The Admin area is used during initial setup to configure the application defaults before handing it off to your family.
FamilyAI runs on a Windows machine and uses Ollama for all AI processing locally. No data leaves your network.
